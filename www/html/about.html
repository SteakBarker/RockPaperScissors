Help

How to use

Once you join a game, you will see 3 things:

The status at the top of the page. It should say something like 'PENDING', 'WON' or 'LOST'. It tells you your current status. Pending means you're waiting on the other player to make their move.

[NAME] vs [NAME]. Your name is always on the left, and on the right is who you're battling.

Personal Link

When you create a game, you are given a personal link. That is a link for your eyes only. Click/Use your personal link to join the game and play. Keep it private, otherwise people can play as you!

The personal link contains two things: GameID, UserID. The userID is what is important -- its how the server verifys you are a player or not, and who you are

Joining A Game

If you're given a link, you can use that link to quickly join. However, if you're just given a GameID (Example:4209), you can use the Join game page and simply enter your GameID and your name and join

Open Games

Open games are games no-one has joined yet. You can view all the open games, and join anyone you fancy
